Hey, I am Chaoran Hu, a statistics Ph.D. produced by University of Connecticut.

It is not easy to find an accurate answer for this question, 'who are you'. Indeed, people always play different roles in life. In UConn Health Center, I am a biostatistics consultant. In UConn, I teach STAT3025: statistics models. When talking about research, I focus on stochastic process and I also develop some R packages.

In life, pour-over coffee and country music are my hobby. I have some freinds, also, but not a lot. I grow up in Anhui, a beautiful place with memory. Four years of youth were spent in Wuhan, to get a bachelor of science in math. Currently, I am living in Indianapolis.
